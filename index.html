Tailored Cleaning Solutions

Every space is different, so we don’t believe in one-size-fits-all pricing. Your quote is based on:

  • Size & layout – number of rooms, storeys and high-touch areas.
  • Type of premises – homes, offices, retail, childcare or medical.
  • Frequency – one-off, weekly, fortnightly, or rostered after-hours.
  • Special requests – carpet steam, window detailing, hygiene fogging, etc.

Tell us what you need and we’ll send a firm, obligation-free quote—usually within the hour.

FAQ & About BeautyClean

BeautyClean is a trusted cleaning company based on the Gold Coast, proudly servicing homes and businesses across Brisbane, Logan, and nearby areas. We specialise in providing reliable, detail-oriented cleaning solutions — whether it’s a family home, retail shop, or commercial office.

Our core services include:
  • Floor Cleaning & Scrubbing – Deep mechanical scrubbing and polishing for tiles, vinyl, and concrete floors to restore shine.
  • Spring Clean – A detailed top-to-bottom clean perfect for pre-inspection or seasonal refreshes.
  • Disinfection / Post-Outbreak Cleaning – Sanitisation using hospital-grade disinfectants to prevent the spread of illness.
  • Office & Commercial Cleaning – After-hours or scheduled cleaning programs for workplaces of all sizes.
  • Carpet & Upholstery Steam Cleaning – Hot-water extraction that removes stains, odours, and allergens.
  • Window & Glass Cleaning – Streak-free cleaning for windows, glass doors, and shopfronts.

We’re based on the Gold Coast and serve clients across Brisbane, Logan, and surrounding suburbs throughout South-East Queensland. Not sure if we cover your area? Just send us a quick message — we’ll confirm right away.

You can request a quote directly through our online form or by phone. Once we confirm your date and quote, you’ll receive a booking confirmation. We accept payments by bank transfer, card, or cash after the service.
